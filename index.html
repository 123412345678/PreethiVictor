01 — Profile

About Me

I am a Senior AML Transaction Monitoring Analyst with hands-on experience across financial crime surveillance, Know Your Customer (KYC) reviews, Customer Due Diligence (CDD), and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) for correspondent and non-correspondent banking relationships. My work sits at the intersection of transaction behaviour, customer risk, and regulatory obligation.

Day to day, I review customer transactions to identify suspicious activity, investigate alerts generated from transaction monitoring systems, and analyze transactional behaviour against expected account activity. This includes reviewing source of funds and source of wealth, assessing ownership structures and business relationships, and conducting risk-based investigations across high-risk jurisdictions and typologies such as structuring and third-party transaction risk.

I escalate suspicious cases in line with internal policy, prepare clear and defensible investigation documentation, and work closely with global compliance teams to ensure adherence to AML regulations. Beyond casework, I contribute to quality assurance — reviewing completed investigations, identifying gaps, and recommending process improvements that raise the standard of the wider team.
